Let’s be real for a second. Kanpur has plenty of spots to grab a quick bite, but absolutely no other café in Kanpur is doing what Meraki Café is doing. We aren't just serving food; we are making a statement.

From our legendary 72-hour cold-fermented Neapolitan pizza to the aesthetic corners that absolutely clear your Instagram feed, everything here hits differently. But the real plot twist? The true soul behind your favorite safe haven is a powerhouse woman and her absolute dream team.

The Ultimate Plot Twist: Meet Richa Tiwari

Behind the counter, curating the vibes, and crafting the recipes is our founder, Richa Tiwari. She isn't just a business owner; she’s a proud mother of two daughters who decided that her lifelong passion for cooking belonged in the spotlight, not just inside her home kitchen.

Richa spent years experimenting with flavor profiles, treating food as her ultimate creative outlet. When she decided to turn that passion into a full-time profession, she didn't want to just build a cool restaurant. As a mother raising young women, she had a vision to build something that literally did not exist anywhere else in Kanpur.

"I wanted to build a space where my own daughters could hang out for hours completely untroubled, feel inspired, and eat insanely good food. So, I built it myself." — Richa Tiwari, Founder

The All-Girls Squad Identity (And Why It Matters)

Here’s the main character energy you won't find anywhere else: Meraki Café is run 100% by an all-girls staff. Every single part of the operation—from the kitchen line tossing our signature crusts to the front-of-house hospitality—is managed entirely by incredible, hard-working girls.

This isn't a marketing gimmick; it’s a standard. This distinct identity automatically makes Meraki the absolute safest café in Saket Nagar for girls, student groups, and families. Whether you're pulling an all-night study session, heading out for a solo coffee date, or hosting a family dinner, the energy here is always protective, respectful, and completely comfortable. You can truly let your guard down and just vibe.
