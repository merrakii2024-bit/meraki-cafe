What Does Meraki Mean?

The name says everything. Meraki (μεράκι) is a Greek word that means doing something with soul, creativity, and absolute love — leaving a piece of yourself in your work. That is the philosophy behind every dish, every drink, and every corner of this café in Saket Nagar, Kanpur.

When you walk in, you feel it immediately. The warm lighting, the relaxed music, the smell of coffee and fresh dough — and of course, Katta and Lichi, the café's two resident dogs who greet every guest like an old friend.

"Meraki is not just a café. It is the kind of place where you come for a quick coffee and stay for three hours because you simply do not want to leave."

The Food — What You Must Order

1. The 72-Hour Cold Fermented Neapolitan Pizza

This is Meraki Café's crown jewel — and arguably the best pizza in Kanpur. The dough is cold-fermented for a full 72 hours using authentic Neapolitan technique. The result? A crust that is impossibly light, crispy on the outside, soft inside, and far easier to digest than regular pizza. Once you have had it, regular pizza feels heavy by comparison.

Available in classics like Margherita, Arrabbiata, Quattro Formaggi, Hawaiian, and Pesto — starting at just ₹350. It is one of those dishes that Kanpur did not know it was missing until Meraki arrived.

2. Stone Cold Rolled Ice Cream

Watch your dessert being made right in front of you. Fresh ingredients poured onto a frozen stone plate, mixed, spread thin, and hand-rolled into perfect tubes. It is part dessert, part live performance — and 100% delicious. Starting at ₹140, this is the dish that puts Meraki Café on Kanpur's map for dessert lovers.

3. Boba Bubble Tea — Kanpur's Newest Love

Meraki Café recently launched Kanpur's most exciting boba menu — classic milk teas, coffee bobas, thick shakes with tapioca pearls, and exclusive Meraki boba mojitos. The Cranberry Sunrise Boba Mojito is already a favourite. If you have not tried boba in Kanpur yet, Meraki is the place to start.

4. Orange Mojito — The Most Ordered Drink

Fresh orange, mint, a squeeze of lime, and sparkling water. Simple, refreshing, and completely addictive. At ₹189, the Orange Mojito is Meraki Café's most ordered drink — and once you taste it, you will understand why.

5. Aglio e Olio Pasta

Silky pasta tossed in premium olive oil, garlic, chilli flakes and fresh parsley. One of Kanpur's most talked-about continental dishes. Deceptively simple, perfectly executed. A fan favourite since day one.

Meet Katta and Lichi — Kanpur's Most Famous Café Dogs

No visit to Meraki Café is complete without meeting Katta and Lichi. These two adorable resident dogs have become as much a part of the Meraki identity as the food itself. The café is fully pet-friendly — so bring your furry friends along too. In a city where truly pet-friendly spaces are rare, Meraki Café stands apart.

Katta and Lichi have their own fan following on Instagram and have been featured in customer posts and stories more times than we can count. They are the unofficial mascots of the best café in Saket Nagar, Kanpur — and they take the job very seriously.

The Vibe — What to Expect

Meraki Café is cozy, warm, and intimate — the kind of place that works equally well for a solo afternoon with a book, a date with someone special, a long catch-up session with friends, or a family outing on a Sunday evening.

The décor is warm and thoughtful. The lighting is soft. The music is curated. And the staff treat every guest like they belong there. It is the kind of café that Kanpur has been wanting for a long time.

Meraki Café is located in Saket Nagar, one of Kanpur's most vibrant residential neighbourhoods. From Kanpur Central Railway Station, it is approximately 20–25 minutes by auto or cab. From Civil Lines, around 15–18 minutes. From Kidwai Nagar, just 10 minutes away.

The easiest way to find us — open Google Maps and search "Meraki Café Kanpur" or "Meraki Café Saket Nagar". We are on the map and easy to find.
