Who We Are

A Hidden Gem in Saket Nagar

Meraki Cafe is Kanpur's cosiest neighbourhood café tucked away in the lanes of Saket Nagar, Juhi Kalan. We believe food is best enjoyed slowly — with good music, warm lighting, and even better company.

From our signature 72-Hour Cold Fermented Neapolitan Pizzas and silky pastas to stone cold & rolled ice creams and freshly brewed coffee, every item is crafted with care. Come hungry, leave happy.

The Soul Behind the Cafe

Born From Passion, Served With Love

Meraki Cafe is not just a restaurant — it is a dream cooked slowly, with love, long before the first plate was ever served. Our founder's love affair with food began in her own kitchen — stirring, tasting, experimenting, and pouring her whole heart into everything she made.

"Meraki — it means to do something with soul, creativity, and love. To leave a piece of yourself in your work. That is exactly what this café is."

When you visit Meraki, you are not just a customer. You are a guest in her world. And she wants you to leave a little happier than when you arrived.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Meraki Café located in Kanpur?

Meraki Café is at 127/624, W1, Juhi Kalan, Keshav Nagar, Saket Nagar, Kanpur – 208014. WhatsApp us at +91 90824 17010 for directions.

What are Meraki Café's opening hours?

We are open every day from 1:00 PM to 10:00 PM. No reservation needed — just walk in!

What is Meraki Café famous for in Kanpur?

We're famous for our 72-hour cold fermented Neapolitan pizza, stone-rolled ice creams, boba drinks, orange mojito, thick milkshakes, and cozy pet-friendly ambiance. Come meet our dogs Katta & Lichi!

Is Meraki Café pet-friendly?

Yes! Meraki Café is fully pet-friendly. Our resident dogs Katta and Lichi love meeting guests and their furry friends.

Is Meraki Café 100% vegetarian?

Yes! Every item on our menu is 100% vegetarian. All prices are inclusive of GST.

What makes your pizza different?

Our pizza dough is cold-fermented for 72 hours using authentic Neapolitan technique — making it lighter, crispier, easier to digest, and far more flavourful than regular pizza. One of the best pizzas in Kanpur!

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Cash, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and all UPI apps. You can also pay directly via our website.

What is the average cost for two?

Average cost for two is ₹300–₹500 — great value for the quality and ambiance we offer in Kanpur.
