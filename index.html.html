Who We Are

A Hidden Gem in Saket Nagar

Meraki Cafe is Kanpur's cosiest neighbourhood café tucked away in the lanes of Saket Nagar, Juhi Kalan. We believe food is best enjoyed slowly — with good music, warm lighting, and even better company.

From our signature 72-Hour Cold Fermented Neapolitan Pizzas and silky pastas to stone cold & rolled ice creams and freshly brewed coffee, every item is crafted with care. Come hungry, leave happy.

The Soul Behind the Cafe

Born From Passion, Served With Love

Meraki Cafe is not just a restaurant — it is a dream cooked slowly, with love, long before the first plate was ever served. Our founder's love affair with food began in her own kitchen — stirring, tasting, experimenting, and pouring her whole heart into everything she made.

"Meraki — it means to do something with soul, creativity, and love. To leave a piece of yourself in your work. That is exactly what this café is."

When you visit Meraki, you are not just a customer. You are a guest in her world. And she wants you to leave a little happier than when you arrived.

— With love, from the heart of Meraki Cafe 🤍
